## Zeta Reticuli System (Alleged Origin)

### Astronomical Data

- Fish, M.H. (1974). Zeta Reticuli star system data.
  - Binary star system, 39.5 light-years from Earth
  - G-type stars similar to our Sun
  - Potentially habitable zone

### Scientific Assessment

- No confirmed exoplanets in Zeta Reticuli system as of 2026
- Distance would require faster-than-light or generation ship technology
- Pure speculation based on Hill star map interpretation
